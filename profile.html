Bio

Dr. Sabir Awad Mustafa is a Consultant in Medical Microbiology and Infectious Diseases at Wadi Aldawasir General Hospital, Saudi Arabia, and an Assistant Professor at the University of Kordofan. He is an active research scientist in the fields of infection control, antimicrobial resistance, and diagnostic microbiology. His research focuses on improving patient safety, preventing healthcare-associated infections, and collaborating internationally for better hospital infection outcomes.
