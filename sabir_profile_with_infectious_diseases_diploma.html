About Me

I am Dr. Sabir Awad Mustafa, a Consultant in Medical Microbiology and Infectious Diseases, and the Director of the Laboratory and Blood Bank at Wadi Aldawasir General Hospital. With over a decade of experience, I specialize in diagnostic microbiology, infection control, and antimicrobial stewardship. I have significantly contributed to developing antibiotic guidelines, reducing healthcare-associated infections, and improving laboratory quality through performance improvement initiatives. In addition to my clinical role, I serve as an Assistant Professor (Collaborator) at the University of Kordofan, where I engage in teaching and mentoring medical and health sciences students. My academic contributions aim to cultivate a new generation of competent and responsible healthcare professionals. Throughout my career, I have combined hands-on clinical expertise with academic involvement, with a strong commitment to enhancing healthcare quality, advancing patient safety, and driving innovation in infection prevention.
